About Us

AYBU Cyber is a cybersecurity club founded by the students of Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University. We are dedicated to creating a supportive environment where future professionals can collaborate, develop practical skills, and stay informed about the latest trends in cyber defense.

Vision

Our vision is to raise awareness among university students about ethical hacking, cybersecurity research, and digital security. By equipping them with cutting-edge knowledge and a responsible approach to technology, we aim to help them navigate today’s interconnected world more confidently and ethically.

Mission

We fulfill our mission by organizing weekly cybersecurity news briefings, publishing in-depth technical articles, and hosting CTF (Capture the Flag) competitions. Through these activities, we ensure that our members gain hands-on experience, sharpen their problem-solving abilities, and build a strong foundation for their future roles in the ever-evolving cybersecurity landscape.
